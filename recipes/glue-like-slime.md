# Glue-like slime
Glue is a sticky, gooey liquid that stretches well. Creating a glue-like slime is difficult, but it can be achieved by devising ways to weaken the crosslinking reaction of borax.

## Materials per 5L of slime
- 6g of borax
- 19g of fructose
- 200g of sodium bicarbonate
- 390g of pregelatinized potato starch
- 120g of Kuraray Poval 44-88 S2 (PVA powder)

## How to make
1. Put fructose, sodium bicarbonate, potato starch, and PVA powder in a bag, shake for about 30 seconds to mix them, and make a powder mixture.
2. Pour 4L of water into the bucket.
3. Put borax into the bottle, add approximately 400mL of water, close the lid, and shake for about 30 seconds to dissolve.
4. Add the powder mixture to the bucket while stirring the water.
5. Gradually add the borax solution to the bucket while mixing.

## How to clean up
1. Put the slime in a bucket, add 15g of fructose, and stir until the slime dissolves.
2. Close the bath plug and pour the dissolved liquid into the bathtub.
3. Add hot water and stir again.
4. Once the viscosity has decreased, pour it down the drain.

## Shelf life
Two weeks
